The Flash by Sarah Chang

TV Show Description

"My name is Barry Allen and I am the fastest man alive." A lightening bolt and a particle accelerator explosion gave Barry the ability to run unbelievably fast. Unfortunately, he wasn't the only one given powers and not everyone uses them for good. Barry works with his team at STAR Labs to save Central City from this new threat.
