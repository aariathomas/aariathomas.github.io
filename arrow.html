Arrow by: Anushka Sanyal

TV Show Description

Spoiled billionaire playboy Oliver Queen is missing and presumed dead when his yacht is lost at sea. He returns five years later a changed man, determined to clean up the city as a hooded vigilante armed with a bow. As his loved ones hang at stake, he tries to save Starling City and risks the entire world finding out his secrets.
